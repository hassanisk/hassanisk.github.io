Paws and Play

I had to build a full-stack app using Node.js and angularJs with a group of four.
From the start, our group had a meeting to brainstorm and discuss the potential ideas. I offered couple of ideas but we ended up going with a dog walking app because, oh well, who doesn't like dogs. After receiving the confirmation from the TA’s, we allocated roles for each one of us based on our strengths.

I decided to work on the front-end using angularJs. Although, I offered a big help when it’s needed and never hesitate to ask for one. During pair programming, I got involved in building the database schemas, navigation and layouts of the user interface since I have some previous skills in information architect from my Master’s degree. Finally, as a part of the group, I used git to share my own work with the team.
