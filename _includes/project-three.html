Paws and Play

We had to build a full-stack app using Node.js and AngularJs in a group of four. Our group began the process with a brainstorming session to discuss any potential ideas. I put forward several ideas however, in the end we decided to develop a dog walking app because firstly, who doesn't like dogs and secondly, it would enable us to introduce some interesting functionality elements. After receiving approval from the Teaching Assistants, we allocated roles based on our relative strengths.

I decided to work on the front-end elements using angularJs. As part of a team, I not only worked on my elements but also helped other team members when they needed help and I learned how to ask for help when needed.
